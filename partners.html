Our Partners

Rahul Gupta

Rahul Gupta is the Founding Partner of Maximus Legal. Rahul is an alumnus of the Faculty of Law, University of Delhi and is also a Company Secretary. Rahul has diverse experience in advising and representing clients across sectors and industries on issues concerning various laws, before various judicial forms, i.e., Supreme Court, High Court, District Courts, National Company Law Tribunal, Real Estate Regulatory Authority, Consumer Forum. Rahul regularly advises clients on pre-litigation strategy and renders legal opinions on complex questions of law. Rahul’s expertise lies in Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, Intellectual Property Rights, Real Estate and Due Diligence.

Deepti Gupta

Deepti Gupta is a partner specializing in Corporate and Commercial Laws. Deepti completed her LL.B. from ILS Law College, Pune in the year 2015. With years of experience and a deep understanding of contract law, Deepti possesses a keen ability to craft comprehensive and tailored contracts that protect the interests of our clients while fostering mutually beneficial business relationships. Deepti's expertise extends across various industries and sectors, enabling her to adeptly handle a wide range of contractual matters. Deepti is dedicated to assisting businesses, entrepreneurs, and organizations in navigating the complex legal landscape surrounding website policies and terms of use. Deepti understands the importance of establishing comprehensive and legally sound policies to protect both businesses and their online users.
